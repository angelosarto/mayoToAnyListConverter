  1. raw (1.8oz) Eggs, 1 large
  2. 1 tbsp(s) (0.5oz) Light mayo
  3. 1½ tbsp(s) (0.5oz) Red onion, chopped
  4. 1 tsp(s) (0.1oz) Cumin, ground
  5. ground Black pepper, 1 dash(es)
  6. juiced (0.7oz) Lemon, ¼ medium
  7. 2 slice(s) (2.3oz) Whole-wheat bread
  8. ¼ cup(s) (1oz) Carrot, shredded
  9. diced (3oz) Cherry tomatoes, 5 cherry tomato(es)
  10. 1 handful(s) (0.8oz) Baby spinach
  11. 1 cup(s) (4oz) Vegetable sticks (carrot, celery, cucumber, bell pepper)
  12. 1 small piece(s) (3.5oz) Fruit, any type
  13. 7 oz(s) (7oz) Non-fat plain Greek yogurt

Place egg in a small saucepan, covering with cold water and bringing to the boil over high heat. Once boiling, immediately reduce heat to medium and gently boil for 8 minutes. Remove from hot water and place in ice cold water until cool.

Remove egg from water, discard shell and mash egg together with mayo, onion, cumin, black pepper, and lemon juice.

Spread one slice of bread with curried egg mixture and top with carrot, tomatoes and spinach. Top with other slice to make a sandwich. Serve with a side of vegetable sticks.

Enjoy a piece of fruit & yogurt for dessert.

TIP: For cooler days, toast sandwich in a sandwich press for a warm lunch option. If preferred, add salad vegetables after toasting. Cook eggs at the start of the week and store in the fridge for use in lunch meals and snacks. Boiled eggs can be kept for up to one week in the fridge, with their shells left on.
