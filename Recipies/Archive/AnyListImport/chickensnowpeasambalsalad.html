To make the sambal bean salad, add snow peas, tomatoes, cucumber, red bell pepper, almonds, cilantro, sweet chilli sauce and lime juice. Gently toss to combine.

Cook rice according to packet instructions, place in a bowl, add spinach and mix in the chicken. Add sambal salad mixture to bowl, season to taste and toss to combine.

TIP: For cooler days, microwave or heat chicken in a frying pan and add to salad for a warm lunch.
