  1. 1 cup(s) (0.7oz) Baby spinach
  2. 1 tbsp(s) (0.4oz) Red onion, chopped
  3. 2½ tbsp(s) (0.8oz) Feta cheese, reduced-fat, crumbled
  4. 2½ tbsp(s) (0.6oz) Cheddar cheese, reduced-fat, shredded
  5. 1 dash(es) Black pepper
  6. 4 tbsp(s) (2.1oz) Avocado
  7. 1 tortilla(s) (1.4oz) Whole-wheat tortilla
  8. pieces (2.5oz) Mushrooms, 1 cup(s)
  9. 1 small piece(s) (3.5oz) Fruit, any type

Preheat a sandwich press.

Combine spinach, onion, feta and cheddar in a bowl. Season with pepper.

Spread the avocado over the tortilla, then top with spinach mixture and mushrooms. Roll to form a wrap.

Place wrap in sandwich press and toast for 5 minutes or until golden.

Enjoy with a piece of fruit or save for your morning snack.
