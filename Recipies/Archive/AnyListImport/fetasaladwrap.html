  1. 1 tortilla(s) (1.4oz) Whole-wheat tortilla
  2. 2 tbsp(s) (1.1oz) Avocado
  3. or arugula if preferred (0.7oz) Baby spinach, 1 cup(s)
  4. sliced (2.8oz) Cucumber, ½ small
  5. 1 cup(s) (5.3oz) Red bell pepper, chopped
  6. 1½ oz(s) (1.5oz) Feta cheese, reduced-fat, crumbled
  7. ¼ cup(s) (1.4oz) Red onion, chopped

Spread the avocado over the tortilla and top with spinach, cucumber, bell pepper, feta and red onion. Roll tightly to make a wrap.

TIP: Enjoy this wrap fresh, or toast in a sandwich press for a warm option.
