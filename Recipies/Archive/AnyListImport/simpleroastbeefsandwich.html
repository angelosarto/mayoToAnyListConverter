  1. 2 slice(s) (2.3oz) Whole-wheat bread
  2. 2 tbsp(s) (1.1oz) Avocado
  3. 3 oz(s) (3oz) Sliced deli beef, reduced-sodium
  4. 1½ tbsp(s) (0.5oz) Red onion, chopped
  5. halved (3oz) Cherry tomatoes, 5 cherry tomato(es)
  6. ribbons or sliced (5.3oz) Cucumber, ½ cucumber(s)
  7. 2½ tbsp(s) (0.8oz) Red bell pepper, chopped
  8. 1 handful(s) (0.8oz) Baby spinach

Spread 1 slice of bread with avocado and top with sliced beef, onion, tomatoes, cucumber, bell pepper and spinach. Top with other slice of bread to make a sandwich.

TIP: For cooler days, toast bread and microwave or heat beef in a frying pan for a warm lunch.
