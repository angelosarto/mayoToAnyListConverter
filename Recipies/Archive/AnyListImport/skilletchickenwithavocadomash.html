  1. ½ medium avocado(s) (3.5oz) Avocado
  2. ¼ cup(s) (2oz) Non-fat plain Greek yogurt
  3. 2 tsp(s) (0.4oz) Red wine vinegar
  4. thinly sliced (0.5oz) Green onion, 1 medium
  5. 1 dash(es) Black pepper
  6. 4 oz(s) (4oz) Chicken breast, raw
  7. 1 cup(s) (1.7oz) Mixed salad leaves
  8. sliced (4.2oz) Red bell pepper, raw, 1 medium

Mash together avocado, yogurt, red wine vinegar, and green onion and season with pepper. Cook chicken in a non-stick skillet over medium-high heat for 3 to 4 minutes on each side or until cooked and golden and reaches an internal temperature of 165°F.

Place salad leaves and bell pepper onto a serving plate, spoon the avocado mash alongside and then top with the chicken, then season with pepper and serve.
