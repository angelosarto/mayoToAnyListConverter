  1. 2 cup(s) (5.6oz) Old fashioned (rolled) oats
  2. 1¼ cup (3.8oz) Dried apple
  3. roughly chopped (1oz) Hazelnuts, 20 nut(s)
  4. ¼ cup(s) (0.7oz) Shredded coconut, unsweetened
  5. 1 tbsp(s) (0.4oz) Chia seeds
  6. 4 cup(s) (2lb) Non-fat plain Greek yogurt

Prepare Easy natural muesli according to recipe instructions and serve a portion for breakfast.

TIP: The Easy natural muesli recipe makes four serves, save leftover portions for use throughout the week.

Easy natural muesli instructions

Combine oats, apple, hazelnuts, coconut and chia seeds in a bowl and mix well. Store in an airtight container to use throughout the week.

To serve, scoop out about 1 cup of muesli and sprinkle over 1 cup of yogurt.

TIP: If preferred, serve muesli with half a cup of milk and half a cup of yogurt.
