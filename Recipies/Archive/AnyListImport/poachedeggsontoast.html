  1. 2 large (3.5oz) Eggs
  2. 2 slice(s) (2.3oz) Whole-grain bread
  3. 4 tbsp(s) (2.1oz) Avocado
  4. 1 dash(es) Black pepper
  5. 1 small piece(s) (3.5oz) Fruit, any type

Add water to a large saucepan until 3 inches deep. Bring to a boil and then reduce to a gentle simmer. Crack one egg into a small bowl. Using a large spoon, gently stir the water in a circular motion to create a whirlpool. Carefully slide the egg into the water. Immediately repeat with the second egg.

Allow the eggs to poach for about 3 to 4 minutes for runny yolks and 4 to 5 minutes for firmer yolks. Remove eggs with a slotted spoon once cooked to your liking.

In the meantime, toast the bread and spread both slices with avocado. Top with poached eggs. Season to taste.

Follow with piece of fresh fruit, or save for a mid-morning snack.
