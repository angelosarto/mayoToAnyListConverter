  1. chopped (1.1oz) Avocado, 2 tbsp(s)
  2. chopped (2.2oz) Tomato, ½ medium, whole
  3. sliced (0.5oz) Green onion, 1 medium
  4. to taste Black pepper, 1 dash(es), ground
  5. 4 crispbread(s) (1.4oz) Rye crispbread
  6. 1 large handful(s) (1.6oz) Baby spinach
  7. ½ can (3oz) Tuna, canned in water, 3 oz(s), drained
  8. 4 tbsp(s) (1oz) Carrot, shredded

In a small bowl, gently mash avocado, tomato, green onion, and black pepper.

Spread avocado mixture on top of crispbreads, followed by baby spinach.

In the same bowl, mix tuna and carrot together and place on top of crispbreads.
