  1. 4 tsp(s) (0.6oz) Extra virgin olive oil
  2. scrubbed and cut lengthways (2.8oz) Baby carrot, 8 medium
  3. cut into thick slices (4.2oz) Red bell pepper, raw, 1 medium
  4. 1⅓ cup(s) (9.9oz) Instant brown rice, cooked
  5. 2 cup(s) (1.3oz) Baby spinach
  6. finely sliced (1.1oz) Green onion, 2 medium
  7. ½ cup(s) (4.2oz) Hummus
  8. roughly chopped (2.1oz) Parsley, fresh, 2 handful(s)
  9. zest and juice (1.5oz) Lemon, ½ medium
  10. ½ tsp(s) Paprika

Prepare Hummus bliss bowl according to recipe instructions below.

Enjoy one serving for lunch and store the remaining rice and vegetables in an airtight container in the fridge for lunch later in the week.

Hummus bliss bowl instructions

Preheat the oven to 350°F. Rub the oil over the carrots and peppers. Add only the carrots to a roasting pan and roast in the oven for 20 minutes. Carefully add the red pepper slices to the pan, and cook for a further 10 minutes. Remove from the oven and set aside to cool.

Prepare the rice according to packet instructions.

Into a bowl, add the rice, spinach, roasted vegetables, and green onion. Top with the hummus, parsley, and lemon zest. Squeeze over the lemon juice and sprinkle with a pinch of paprika prior to serving.
