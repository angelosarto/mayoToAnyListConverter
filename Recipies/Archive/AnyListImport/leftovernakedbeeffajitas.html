To make salsa, mix together cucumber, tomato, coriander, lime juice and pepper in a bowl and set aside.

Meanwhile, reheat a leftover portion of the Naked Beef Fajitas recipe.

Spoon heated up mixture over two romaine lettuce leaves and top with salsa.

Fold romaine leaves and eat like a taco (be careful of spills!). Alternatively, shred lettuce leaves and enjoy as a salad.
