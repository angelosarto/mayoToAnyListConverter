  1. 2 tbsp(s) (1.1oz) Avocado
  2. 2 slice(s) (2.3oz) Whole-grain bread
  3. ⅓ cup(s) (1.3oz) Cheddar cheese, reduced-fat, shredded
  4. 1½ tbsp(s) (0.5oz) Red onion, chopped
  5. inner (0.4oz) Romaine lettuce, 2 leaf(s)
  6. halved (3oz) Cherry tomatoes, 5 cherry tomato(es)

Lightly mash avocado and season to taste.

Spread avocado mixture on one slice of bread. Add cheese, onion, lettuce, and tomato.  

Place other slice of bread on top to make a sandwich.

TIP: For cooler days, toast bread before adding ingredients or toast sandwich in sandwich press for a warm lunch.
