  1. 6 oz(s) (6oz) Chicken breast, raw
  2. 1 tbsp(s) (0.6oz) Sweet chili sauce, low-sodium
  3. 1 bunch(es) (4.2oz) Baby bok choy
  4. 4 tsp(s) (0.6oz) Extra virgin olive oil
  5. ⅓ cup(s) (1.9oz) Red onion, chopped
  6. chopped (3.5oz) Green beans, trimmed, 1 cup(s)
  7. ½ cup(s) (2.6oz) Red bell pepper, chopped
  8. Thai or sweet basil (0.4oz) Basil, fresh, ½ cup(s), whole leaves
  9. 2 tsp(s) (0.2oz) Sesame seeds

Prepare Sweet chili chicken & basil stir-fry according to recipe instructions below. Enjoy one serving for dinner.

Refrigerate the remaining serving in an airtight container for later in the week. Keep the sesame seeds and basil in separate containers and only add once ready to eat.

Sweet chili chicken & basil stir-fry instructions

Thinly slice the chicken, place into a bowl. Spoon over half the sweet chili sauce, stir to coat.

Chop the bok choy leaves and place onto a plate. Thinly slice the bok choy base.

Heat 1 tablespoon of the oil in a wok over high heat. Add the chicken, stir-fry 1 minute until golden, remove to a plate. Reduce heat to medium-high. Add the onion and 1 tablespoon water, stir-fry for 30 seconds, until soft. Add the remaining 1 teaspoon oil, green beans and bok choy base and stir-fry, stirring to coat in the oil and combine the vegetables. Add another 2 teaspoons water, cover the wok and wait 30 seconds.

Remove the lid, add the bell pepper and return the chicken. Add the remaining sweet chili sauce, stir-fry for 1 minute until chicken is warmed through. Stir through the chopped bok choy leaves. Remove from heat.

Just before serving, add the basil and sesame seeds, stir to combine. Divide between two serving bowls and serve.

TIP: You can replace the sweet chili sauce with chili jam stir-fry paste, which is available in the Asian sauce section at the supermarket.
