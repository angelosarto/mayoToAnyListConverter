  1. sliced lengthways (4.3oz) Carrots, 2 medium
  2. chopped (1.4oz) Baby broccoli (broccolini), ½ cup(s)
  3. 1 tsp(s) (0.2oz) Extra virgin olive oil
  4. 1 tsp(s) (0.1oz) Curry powder
  5. 3 oz(s) (3oz) Whitefish, raw

Preheat oven to 425°F.

Toss carrots and broccolini in ½ tsp of olive oil and the curry powder. When oven is heated, roast carrots for 20 minutes or until carrots are caramelized and tender. Roast broccolini for 10 minutes. Season to taste.

Meanwhile, heat remaining oil in a non-stick frying pan over medium to high heat.

Season fish and cook for 4 to 5 minutes each side or until cooked through.

To serve, plate fish with carrots and broccolini.

TIP: Season fish with pepper or any other seasoning you like for example, cumin, paprika or lemon and garlic. Add lemon wedges, to serve.
