  1. 4.4 oz(s) (4.4oz) Instant brown rice, cooked
  2. approx ½ cup (2oz) Chicken breast, cooked, 2 oz(s), shredded
  3. ¼ cup(s) (1oz) Carrot, shredded
  4. diced (3.5oz) Cucumber, ½ medium
  5. sliced (0.5oz) Red onion, ¼ small
  6. ½ cup(s) (0.4oz) Arugula
  7. 1 tsp(s) (0.1oz) Shelled pumpkin seeds
  8. chopped (0.1oz) Fresh mint, 2 tbsp(s)
  9. 1 tbsp(s) (0.5oz) Light mayo
  10. 1 tsp(s) (0.2oz) Red wine vinegar
  11. 1 dash(es) Black pepper

Prepare the rice according to packet instructions.

Place rice into a serving bowl and add chicken, carrot, cucumber, onion, arugula, pumpkin seeds, mint, mayo and vinegar. Season to taste and mix well.

TIP: Swap mayo for avocado if you prefer. Feel free to sprinkle some Moroccan seasoning through the salad for some extra flavor.
