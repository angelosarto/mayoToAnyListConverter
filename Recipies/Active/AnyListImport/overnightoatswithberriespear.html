  1. 2 cup(s) (5.6oz) Old fashioned (rolled) oats
  2. 2 cup(s) (1pt) 1% milk
  3. shredded (5.2oz) Pear, 1 small
  4. 1½ tsp(s) (0.1oz) Ground nutmeg
  5. 2 cup(s) (16oz) Non-fat plain Greek yogurt
  6. chopped (1.4oz) Walnuts, 20 walnut(s)
  7. 2 tbsp(s) (0.6oz) Sesame seeds
  8. frozen or fresh (13.9oz) Frozen berries, any type, 2 cup(s)

Prepare the Overnight oats with berries & pear according to recipe instructions below. 

Portion into 4 separate containers or jars and refrigerate overnight so that you can grab and go in the morning.

When ready to eat, grab one container or jar. Spoon into a bowl, top with ½ cup berries and a quarter of the nut and seed mix. Sprinkle with a pinch of nutmeg if desired.

TIP: For cooler days, warm overnight oats in the microwave.

Overnight oats with berries & pear instructions

Place oats into a bowl or container. Add the milk, pear, nutmeg and yogurt. Stir to combine. Cover and place in the fridge overnight to soak.

To toast walnuts and sesame seeds, heat a small skillet over medium heat. Toast, stirring 2 to 3 minutes until golden being careful not to burn. Remove skillet from heat and allow to cool. Chop walnuts into small pieces. Store nut and seed mix in the fridge in a small airtight container.

When ready to eat, divide oat mixture between four bowls. Top each bowl with half a cup berries and a quarter of the toasted nut and seed mix. Sprinkle with a pinch of nutmeg if desired.

TIP: Prepared overnight oats mix will keep for 5 days in airtight containers in the fridge. Add remaining ingredients when ready to eat. For the nut mix, store in an airtight container in the fridge.
