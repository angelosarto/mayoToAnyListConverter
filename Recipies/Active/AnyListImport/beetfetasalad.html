  1. 4.4 oz(s) (4.4oz) Instant brown rice, cooked
  2. 1 cup(s) (0.7oz) Arugula
  3. sliced (3.5oz) Beets, cooked, 2 small beet(s)
  4. 2 tbsp(s) (0.1oz) Fresh mint
  5. ¼ cup(s) (1.3oz) Feta cheese, reduced-fat, crumbled
  6. handful (1.1oz) Parsley, fresh, ½ cup(s)
  7. 1 tsp(s) (0.2oz) Red wine vinegar
  8. 1 dash(es) Black pepper
  9. 1 tbsp(s) (0.3oz) Shelled pumpkin seeds

Prepare rice according to instructions.

Add rice into a serving bowl along with arugula, beet, mint, feta and parsley. Drizzle with vinegar and season to taste. Gently mix.

Sprinkle with seeds to serve.

TIP: You can purchase pre-cooked beets in most supermarkets.
