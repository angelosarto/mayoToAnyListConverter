Mediterranean grain salad with feta instructions

Boil, steam, or microwave broccoli. Refresh in a bowl of iced water. Drain well. Place into a bowl.

Prepare rice according to packet instructions.

Add the cooked rice, roasted peppers, tomatoes, spinach, cucumber, onion, avocado, capers, almonds, and feta to the bowl with the broccoli. Squeeze over lemon juice and toss gently to combine. Divide between two serving bowls.
