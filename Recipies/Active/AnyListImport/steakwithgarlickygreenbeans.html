  7. 1 tsp(s) (0.1oz) Sesame seeds

Season steak to taste.

Heat a non-stick frying pan on medium-high heat and cook steak for 3 to 4 minutes each side, or until cooked to your liking. Remove from pan and cover.

Clean frying pan and heat to medium-high. Add olive oil, garlic, ginger and green beans. Stir fry for 1 to 2 minutes. Turn off heat and add onions and sesame seeds. Continue to toss for another 30 seconds.

On a serving plate, place green beans on a bed of arugula and serve steak along side.

TIP: Slice the steak into strips and toss with arugula and beans to enjoy as a salad.
