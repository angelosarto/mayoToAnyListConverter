  1. ½ cup(s) (1.4oz) Old fashioned (rolled) oats
  2. 1 tbsp(s) (0.4oz) Chia seeds
  3. ½ cup(s) (4.1fl oz) 1% milk
  4. ½ cup(s) (4oz) Non-fat plain Greek yogurt
  5. sliced (2.4oz) Banana, ½ large
  6. chopped (0.3oz) Almonds, unsalted, 6 almond(s)

Combine oats, chia seeds and milk in a bowl.

Heat in the microwave for 2 minutes on high, or stir in a pan over a hot stove until oats reach a smooth consistency. You can add extra water for a thinner cosistency if you prefer. 

Stir in the yogurt and top with banana and almonds.

TIP: Adding yogurt into the oats will make it creamier and pack an extra protein punch. If preferred, serve on the side with half the banana, or save for a mid morning snack.
