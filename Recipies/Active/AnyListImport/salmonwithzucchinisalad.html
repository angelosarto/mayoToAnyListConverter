  1. 3 oz(s) (3oz) Atlantic salmon, farmed
  2. 1 tsp(s) (0.2oz) Extra virgin olive oil
  3. 1 tsp(s) (0.1oz) Garlic, minced
  4. ribbons or shredded (3.5oz) Zucchini, ½ medium
  5. thin rounds (3.5oz) Cucumber, ½ medium
  6. halved (3oz) Cherry tomatoes, 5 cherry tomato(es)
  7. finely chopped (1.4oz) Celery, 1 stalk(s), medium
  8. 2 handful(s) (1.7oz) Baby spinach
  9. thinly sliced (0.8oz) Red onion, ¼ medium
  10. chopped (0.1oz) Basil, fresh, 1 tbsp(s)
  11. 2 tsp(s) (0.4oz) Red wine vinegar
  12. juiced (0.7oz) Lemon, ¼ medium
  13. 1 dash(es) Black pepper

Pat the salmon skin dry using a paper towel. Rub with oil and garlic. Set aside.

To make the salad, in a small bowl add zucchini, cucumber, tomatoes, celery, spinach, onion, basil leaves, vinegar and lemon juice. Season to taste with pepper and mix gently.

Heat a skillet over a medium-high heat. Cook the salmon skin-side down in the hot pan for 3 to 4 minutes. Carefully flip over and cook for another 3 to 4 minutes, or to your liking.

Serve salmon alongside zucchini salad for a quick and easy dinner.
