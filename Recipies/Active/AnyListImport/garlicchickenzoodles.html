  1. 1 tsp(s) (0.2oz) Extra virgin olive oil
  2. 2 tsp(s) (0.2oz) Garlic, minced
  3. 1 tsp(s) (0.1oz) Ginger, minced
  4. cut into ½ inch pieces (3oz) Chicken breast, raw, 3 oz(s)
  5. ends trimmed (2.8oz) Snow peas, ½ cup(s)
  6. (1/2 bunch) (5.3oz) Asparagus, 10 spear(s)
  7. 2 cup(s) (6.4oz) Zucchini, noodles
  8. 1 dash(es) Black pepper

Heat a large non-stick frying pan or wok on medium-high heat. Add olive oil, garlic, ginger and chicken. Cook for 5-6 minutes or until golden and cooked through.

Add snow peas and asparagus and stir fry for another 1-2 minutes.

Toss in zoodles and cook for 1 minute then turn off heat. Spoon into bowl to serve and season to taste.

TIP: If you have a spiralizer you can prepare zucchini noodles yourself. Add fresh chilli or chilli flakes with the chicken for an extra kick!
