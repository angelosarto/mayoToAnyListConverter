Prepare the Tuna & roast vegetable salad according to recipe instructions below.

Enjoy one serving for lunch. Store the remaining serving in an airtight container and refrigerate for later in the week.

Tuna & roast vegetable salad instructions

Preheat oven to 425°F and line a baking sheet with parchment paper.

Place the sweet potato, eggplant, zucchini and bell pepper on the prepared baking sheet. Drizzle with olive oil and season to taste. Toss to coat. Roast the vegetables for 20 minutes or until cooked and caramelized. Allow to cool and then transfer to a serving bowl.

Meanwhile, to make the dressing, whisk together yogurt, mustard, white wine vinegar and parsley in a bowl and season to taste with pepper.

Into the bowl with the cooked vegetables, add the cooked couscous and tuna. Drizzle over the dressing and toss gently to combine. To serve, divide the salad between two bowls.

TIP: For cooler days, serve roast vegetables and couscous warm.
