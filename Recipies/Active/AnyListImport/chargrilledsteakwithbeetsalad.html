  1. 1 tsp(s) (0.2oz) Extra virgin olive oil
  2. 3 oz(s) (3oz) Beef tenderloin steak, any grade, very lean
  3. ½ cup(s) (0.4oz) Arugula
  4. sliced (3.5oz) Beets, cooked, 2 small beet(s)
  5. ¼ cup(s) (1oz) Carrot, shredded
  6. chopped into wedges (4.3oz) Tomato, 1 medium, whole
  7. sliced (0.5oz) Red onion, ¼ small
  8. 1 tbsp(s) (0.1oz) Fresh mint
  9. 1 tsp(s) (0.2oz) Red wine vinegar
  10. 1 dash(es) Black pepper

Heat oil in a skillet over medium heat.

Season steak and cook, for 3 to 4 minutes each side for medium, or longer if desired. Remove the steak and cover to rest for a few minutes.

In a bowl mix together arugula, beet, carrot, tomato, onion, mint and vinegar and spoon onto serving plate.

Place beef alongside salad and season to taste.

TIP: You can purchase pre-cooked beets in most supermarkets.
