  1. 2 tsp(s) (0.3oz) Extra virgin olive oil
  2. dried Paprika, ½ tsp(s)
  3. ½ tsp(s) Cumin, ground
  4. chopped (1.1oz) Parsley, fresh, ½ cup(s)
  5. 1 tsp(s) (0.1oz) Garlic, minced
  6. ground Black pepper, 1 dash(es)
  7. 4 oz(s) (4oz) Beef tenderloin steak, any grade, very lean
  8. 2 handful(s) (1.7oz) Baby spinach
  9. halved (3oz) Cherry tomatoes, 5 cherry tomato(es)
  10. cut into thin strips (1oz) Red bell pepper, raw, ¼ medium
  11. 4 tbsp(s) (1oz) Carrot, shredded
  12. ⅓ cup(s) (0.9oz) Grated Parmesan cheese
  13. 1 tbsp(s) (0.6oz) Balsamic vinegar
  14. 1 slice(s) (1.1oz) Whole-grain bread

In a small bowl, combine the oil, paprika, cumin, half the parsley, garlic and black pepper and mix well.

Place the beef in a shallow bowl. Pour over the marinade and toss to coat. If you have time, cover and place in the fridge for 30 mins to marinate.

Heat a non-stick frying pan to medium-high heat and cook steak for 3 to 4 minutes each side, or until cooked to your liking. Remove from the pan and cover.

Meanwhile prepare a quick salad using the spinach, tomatoes, bell pepper and carrot. Sprinkle remaining parsley, parmesan and drizzle with balsamic vinegar.

Serve spiced steak alongside salad and a slice of bread.

TIP: Serve steak with steamed mixed vegetables if preferred.

If you have time available make croutons by toasting bread and allowing to cool. Cut into squares and toss through the salad.
