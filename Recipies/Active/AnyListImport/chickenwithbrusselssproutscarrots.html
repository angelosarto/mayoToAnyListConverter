  1. halved (5.4oz) Brussels sprouts, 8 sprout(s)
  2. 1 tsp(s) (0.2oz) Extra virgin olive oil
  3. cut into rounds (2.2oz) Carrots, 1 medium
  4. sliced (0.5oz) Red onion, Â¼ small
  5. 1 tsp(s) (0.1oz) Garlic, minced
  6. diced (3oz) Chicken breast, raw, 3 oz(s)
  7. 1 tsp(s) (0.1oz) Shelled pumpkin seeds
  8. to taste Black pepper, 1 dash(es)

In a bowl of boiling water, blanch the Brussels sprouts for 30 seconds. Drain and set aside.

Heat half of the olive oil in a large skillet or wok to medium heat. Add Brussels sprouts and carrot. Stir fry for 5 to 6 minutes or until vegetables begin to soften. Once softened, place in a bowl and cover.

Add remaining oil to pan, turn heat up to medium-high and add onion and garlic. Cook for 1 minute or until fragrant.

Add chicken and cook for 8 to 10 minutes or until golden and cooked through. Once cooked, add cooked vegetables back into pan and stir fry for 2 minutes and remove from heat.

Spoon into a bowl to serve, sprinkle with pumpkin seeds and season to taste.

TIP: To save time, cook double this recipe and refrigerate one portion in an airtight container to reheat for dinner later in the week.
