  1. 3½ cup(s) (11.3oz) Zucchini, noodles
  2. 6 oz(s) (6oz) Ground pork, lean, raw
  3. raw (1.8oz) Eggs, 1 large
  4. smoked (0.1oz) Paprika, 1 tsp(s), dried
  5. 1 tsp(s) (0.1oz) Cumin, ground
  6. 2 tsp(s) (0.1oz) Dried mixed herbs
  7. finely chopped (0.3oz) Parsley, fresh, 2 tbsp(s)
  8. 4 tsp(s) (0.6oz) Extra virgin olive oil
  9. ⅓ cup(s) (1.9oz) Red onion, chopped
  10. 1 small can (14.5oz) Tomatoes, canned, 14½ oz(s)
  11. to taste Black pepper, 1 dash(es), ground

Prepare Pork meatballs on zoodles according to recipe instructions.

Serve one portion of the recipe for dinner tonight and transfer second portion to an airtight container and refrigerate for use later in the week.

Pork meatballs on zoodles instructions

Preheat the oven to 350°F.

Bring a small pot of water to the boil, and cook the zoodles for 1 to 2 minutes until soft. Strain, and leave to one side.

Combine the ground pork in a bowl with egg, paprika, cumin, half the dried mixed herbs and half of the fresh parsley. Shape into balls and place on a baking tray lined with parchment paper or foil. Bake for 15 to 20 minutes, or until cooked through. Remove from the oven and place to one side.

Meanwhile, heat oil in a frying pan over a medium heat. Add the onion and saute for 2 to 3 minutes or until transparent. Add tomatoes in to the pan and reduce the heat. Then add the remaining dried mixed herbs and fresh parsley, stir together, and bring to the simmer. Add the meatballs to the sauce, and continue to cook until sauce is reduced.

Divide the zoodles between two serving bowls, add the meatballs and sauce over the top, and sprinkle with black pepper.
