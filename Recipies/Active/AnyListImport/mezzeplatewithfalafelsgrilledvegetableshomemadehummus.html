Mezze plate with falafel, grilled vegetables & homemade hummus instructions

Preheat the broiler to medium-high, then place the bell pepper, asparagus and zucchini on a baking tray and cook for 5 to 6 minutes, turning halfway through, then place to one side and leave to cool. Turn the oven to 350°F.

To make the falafel, in a food processor, blitz 1 cup of the chickpeas, 1 tablespoons of olive oil, ¼ of the lemon juiced, fresh cilantro, psyllium and garlic, as well as the cumin, allspice, dried cilantro and cayenne. Taste, and season with black pepper if desired. Form the mix into 6 small falafels, roughly the same size, and bake for 30 minutes, or until cooked through.

To make the homemade chunky hummus, mash ½ cup of the chickpeas in a small bowl. Add ¼ of the lemon juiced and zested, paprika and 1 tablespoon of olive oil. Alternatively blitz everything in a food processor until it has a chunky 'dip' consistency.

Enjoy 2 falafels and a third of the hummus and vegetable for dinner. Refrigerate the remaining two servings.
