  1. 4 tsp(s) (0.6oz) Extra virgin olive oil
  2. ⅔ cup(s) (3.7oz) White onion, chopped
  3. 1 tsp(s) (0.1oz) Garlic, minced
  4. 5 sprigs (0.1oz) Thyme, fresh, 2 tsp(s)
  5. chopped (4.8oz) Eggplant, ¼ eggplant(s), unpeeled
  6. ⅓ cup(s) (1.7oz) Red bell pepper, chopped
  7. diced (6.9oz) Zucchini, 1 medium
  8. 1 can(s) (6.7oz) Tomatoes, canned
  9. rinsed (6.3oz) Lentils, canned, drained, 1 cup(s)
  10. 1 dash(es) Black pepper
  11. whole (5.9oz) Portabella mushrooms, 2 piece(s)
  12. 4 cup(s) (2.8oz) Arugula

Prepare Lentils with roast mushrooms & Mediterranean vegetables according to recipe instructions below.

Enjoy one serving for dinner. Store the remaining serving in an airtight container and refrigerate for later in the week.

Lentils with roast mushrooms & Mediterranean vegetables instructions

Preheat oven 400°F. Lightly grease a medium roasting pan.

Heat 2 teaspoons of olive oil in a medium non-stick skillet over medium heat. Add onion and garlic, cook stirring for 3 minutes until soft. Stir in the thyme, eggplant, bell pepper and zucchini. Cook stirring for 2 minutes. Add tomatoes and 1/3 cup of water. Bring to a simmer. Simmer for 3 to 4 minutes until ratatouille thickens slightly. Remove from the heat, stir in the lentils. Season to taste. Spoon into the roasting pan.

Nestle the mushrooms in the ratatouille. Roast for 15 minutes until mushrooms are just tender. Divide into two portions. Spoon remaining 2 teaspoons of oil over the arugula, toss to coat. Scatter the arugula over the mushrooms. Season to taste. Serve.

TIP: If you prefer, you can swap the lentils for 2 poached eggs and serve them on top of the mushrooms and ratatouille.
