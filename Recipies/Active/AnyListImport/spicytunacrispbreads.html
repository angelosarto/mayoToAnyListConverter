  1. drained (3oz) Tuna, canned in water, 3 oz(s)
  2. 1 tsp(s) (0.1oz) Moroccan seasoning
  3. 4 crispbread(s) (1.4oz) Rye crispbread
  4. chopped (2.2oz) Tomato, ½ medium, whole
  5. mashed (1.1oz) Avocado, 2 tbsp(s)
  6. ¼ cup(s) (1oz) Carrot, shredded
  7. chopped (3.5oz) Cucumber, ½ medium
  8. 1½ tbsp(s) (0.5oz) Red onion, chopped
  9. 1 tsp(s) (0.2oz) Red wine vinegar
  10. 1 dash(es) Black pepper

Mix tuna and seasoning in a bowl and spread evenly over crispbreads.

Mix tomato, avocado, carrot, cucumber, onion and vinegar together.

Spread tomato mixture over tuna on crispbreads. Season to taste.

TIP: If you are unable to find Moroccan seasoning in the supermarket, you can make your own using this recipe: Moroccan spice blend.
