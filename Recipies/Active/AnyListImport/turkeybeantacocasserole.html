  1. 2 tbsp(s) (1oz) Extra virgin olive oil
  2. 6 oz(s) (6oz) Extra lean ground turkey, raw
  3. chopped (2.1oz) Red onion, 1 small
  4. chopped (2oz) Green bell pepper, ½ pepper(s)
  5. chopped (2.1oz) Red bell pepper, raw, ½ medium
  6. chopped (4.2oz) Zucchini, 1 small
  7. 2 tbsp(s) (0.6oz) Taco seasoning
  8. rinsed and drained (2.1oz) Red kidney beans, ⅓ cup(s)
  9. 2 cup(s) (1.1lb) Low sodium chicken stock
  10. 2 cup(s) (6.9oz) Cauliflower rice
  11. 1 cup(s) (0.8oz) Cilantro, fresh

Prepare Turkey & bean taco casserole according to recipe instructions below.

Store the cooked turkey and vegetables in a container in the fridge. Store the remaining cauliflower rice and cilantro in separate airtight containers in the fridge for later in the week.

Turkey & bean taco casserole instructions

Heat the oil in a deep non-stick skillet over medium-high heat and add the turkey and onion. Cook, stirring and breaking up any ground turkey lumps with the back of a spoon, for 3 minutes or until the onion is softened but not browned.

Stir in the bell peppers, zucchini, seasoning, beans and stock and bring to a simmer. Simmer, partially covered and stirring occasionally, for 10 minutes. Remove the lid, continue to simmer, uncovered and stirring occasionally, for another 10 minutes or until the vegetables are tender and the stock has reduced by two-thirds.

Heat the cauliflower rice according to packet instructions. Divide the cauliflower rice and casserole between bowls. Top with the cilantro and serve.
