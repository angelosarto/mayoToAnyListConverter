  1. 1 tbsp(s) (0.6oz) Sweet chili sauce, low-sodium
  2. 1 tsp(s) (0.2oz) Soy sauce, low-sodium
  3. 1 lime(s) (2.4oz) Lime
  4. 2 tsp(s) (0.3oz) Extra virgin olive oil
  5. 3 oz(s) (3oz) Ground beef, extra lean
  6. chopped (1.9oz) Snow peas, ⅓ cup(s)
  7. ½ cup(s) (1.9oz) Carrot, shredded
  8. chopped (0.2oz) Green onion, 1 small, green and white parts
  9. ¼ cup(s) (1.1oz) Bean sprouts
  10. ¼ cup(s) (0.2oz) Cilantro, fresh

In a small bowl, combine the sweet chili sauce, soy sauce and juice of half a lime. Remove ½ tablespoon of the sweet chili soy mixture into a second small bowl and stir in 1 teaspoon of olive oil and reserve for the dressing. Cover both bowls and set aside.

Heat a medium non-stick skillet or wok over medium-high heat. Add remaining 1 teaspoon of olive oil and ground beef. Cook, stirring for 2 minutes, until beef has browned. Add the sweet chili soy mixture (not the dressing) and stir-fry for 1 minute. Remove from the heat, stir through the snow peas, carrots and green onions.

Spoon into a bowl, top with bean sprouts and cilantro. Drizzle the reserved dressing over the bliss bowl. Serve with lime wedges.
