Dukkah-crusted chicken instructions

For the dukkah, scatter the sesame seeds in a small non-stick skillet over medium heat. Cook, shaking skillet for 2 to 3 minutes until the sesame seeds start to color. Add the cumin, coriander, paprika and red pepper flakes, cook while gently shaking the skillet for 1 minute, until spices are warm. Allow to cool.

Place the whisked egg into a bowl and the dukkah into a second bowl. Dip the chicken in the egg and then coat in the dukkah.

Heat the oil in a non-stick skillet over medium heat. Cook the chicken, gently turning after 4 minutes. Cook for a further 3 minutes, or until cooked through and transfer to a plate to rest. Meanwhile, steam the broccoli, carrots and green beans for 3 to 4 miutes, or until cooked to your liking. Season to taste and serve alongside the cooked chicken.

TIP: Make a few servings of the dukkah recipe and store in a jar with a lid.
